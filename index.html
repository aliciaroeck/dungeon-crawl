How to Play:

  • There are four rooms. You will move through each room and be given scenrios in which the you will have to roll a dice to see if you take damage or not.
  • If the you roll between 1-10 you will take damage. Anything between 11-20 and you will make it through the room safely.
  • In the first room the damage taken will be 20 points and will increase by 20 as you move through each room.
  • Good Luck! You'll need it to survive the Dungeon!
